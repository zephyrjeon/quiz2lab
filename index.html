Immutable Infrastructure with AWS

Immutable infrastructure is a modern approach to managing servers where servers are never modified after they are deployed. Instead of updating or patching existing servers, you replace them with new versions whenever changes are required. This approach enhances stability and security by ensuring that all deployments are consistent and predictable.
AWS (Amazon Web Services) supports immutable infrastructure with its robust suite of services.
